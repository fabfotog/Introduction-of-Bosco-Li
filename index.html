    June 2011

    In June 2011, I attained Bachelor Degree of Journalism and Communication from Hong Kong Shue Yan University, and started working as reporter in Commercial Broadcast Co. Ltd. as news reporter, covering different categories of news, including local, politics, finance and sports.

  2. Further Pursue of Knowledge

  3. Job Promotion
